Frequently Asked Questions

How does AI workout generation work?+
During onboarding you tell Nordyx your primary goal (build muscle, lose fat, get stronger, improve endurance, and more), your experience level, how many days per week you want to train, your preferred session length, and whether you train at home or in a gym. This profile is sent to our backend which uses OpenAI to generate a structured, evidence-based plan with progressive overload built in. Your name, email, and workout history are never sent to OpenAI.
Can I try Nordyx for free?+
Yes. You can generate one AI workout plan without creating an account — no sign-in needed. After your free plan you'll need to sign in with Apple to generate more and to save workout history.
Free 1 AI plan per device · No account required · Try before you commit
Starter A new AI plan every week · Unlimited workout tracking · AI coaching · Full session history
What exercise types does Nordyx support?+
Nordyx generates plans with four exercise types, each tracked with the right inputs in the app:
StrengthSets, reps & weight — e.g. barbell squat, bench press, deadlift
BodyweightSets & reps, no equipment — e.g. pull-ups, dips, push-ups, pistol squats
CardioDuration & pace — e.g. treadmill, rower, assault bike
ConditioningHIIT circuits, supersets & finishers — tracked as timed or rep-based blocks
What does the AI coach do?+
The AI coach is a conversational assistant built into the app. Ask it anything training-related — form cues for specific exercises, how to swap an exercise for different equipment, how to adjust volume if you're tired or sore, or how to keep progressing your lifts. The AI coach is available on the Starter plan.
How do I cancel my subscription?+
Subscriptions are managed entirely through Apple — Nordyx never handles your payment details directly. To cancel, go to Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions on your iPhone, tap Nordyx, and tap Cancel Subscription. You keep access until the end of your current billing period. No refunds are given for unused time, in line with Apple's standard policy.
Is my data private? What do you collect?+
We take privacy seriously. Here's exactly what we do and don't collect:

We collect: your fitness profile (goal, experience, schedule), Apple Sign In token, a random device ID to enforce the free plan limit, and your subscription status.

We do NOT collect: your Apple ID email (unless you share it), advertising identifiers (IDFA), or third-party analytics data.

Your workout history is stored locally on your device only — we never have access to your logs. See our full Privacy Policy for details.
My workout history disappeared — what do I do?+
Workout history is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. If you signed out and back in with the same Apple ID, your data should still be there. If you reinstalled the app, iOS may have cleared local data. If you're still having trouble, contact us below — include your Apple ID email and a description of what happened and we'll help investigate.
